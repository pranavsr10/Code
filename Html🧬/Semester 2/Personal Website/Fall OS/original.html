What is Fall-OS?

Fall-OS is just another arch-based Linux distribution. It uses i3wm and lightweight applications, which makes it super fast and light-weight.

  • Download

Features

  • Minimal

    Fall-OS is very minimal and lightweight. It consumes under 500Mb of memory as it only uses i3-window managers and some lightweight applications.

  • Stunning

    Flat and pastel colors, the consistency among all the UI elements, And various themes make Fall-OS look very clean, minimal and Stunning.

  • Powerful

    With built-in support for AUR, Chaotic AUR and being lightweight, Fall-OS gives you the power to do whatever you want on your Linux PC.
